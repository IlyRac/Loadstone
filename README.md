# 🧭 Load Stone - Chunk Loader Mod

**Turn Lodestones into chunk loaders by interacting with them using iron, diamond, or netherite!**

---

## 🎯 What Does This Mod Do?

Tired of your farms and machines stopping when you walk away? **Load Stone** transforms vanilla Lodestones into functional chunk loaders, keeping your world active even when you're not there!

---

## 🎮 How to Use

### ⚡ Activation:
1. **Place a Lodestone** anywhere in your world
2. **Hold** your desired material in hand:
   - ⚒️ **Iron Ingot** for 1×1 chunk
   - 💎 **Diamond** for 3×3 chunks  
   - 🔥 **Netherite Ingot** for 7×7 chunks
3. **Right-click** the Lodestone to activate

### 🔄 Deactivation:
- **Right-click** the active Lodestone with **any item** (or empty hand) to deactivate
- Your original material will be returned to you!

### ⬆️ Changing Tiers:
- **Right-click** with a **different material** to upgrade/downgrade
- Old material is returned, new material is consumed

## ✨ Features

![Raduis](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/cc1b337c47e842aafe5f95d14cc6ec16e70678d0_0.webp)

- **⚡ Tiered Chunk Loading**: Choose from three coverage levels
  - ⚒️ **Iron**: 1×1 chunk (basic coverage)
  - 💎 **Diamond**: 3×3 chunks (standard coverage)
  - 🔥 **Netherite**: 7×7 chunks (massive coverage)

![Activating Particles](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/925dc5b85b1c8216dcbb55e2db345a4149e9b9e9.jpeg)

- **🔄 Smart Material System**: Items are consumed on activation and returned on deactivation
- **🎨 Visual Feedback**: Unique particle effects for each tier
- **⚡ Instant Activation**: Changes take effect immediately
- **🔄 Easy Upgrades**: Switch between tiers anytime by using different materials
